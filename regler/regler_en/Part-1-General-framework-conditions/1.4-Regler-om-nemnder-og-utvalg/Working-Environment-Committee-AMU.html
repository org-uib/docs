Working Environment Committee (AMU)

Norwegian version

The working environment committee shall make efforts to establish a fully satisfactory working environment at UiB. The employer and the employees have an equal number of representatives in the committee and the committee chair alternates between employer and the employees every second year.

The committee has two permanent observers from UiBs Occupational Health Service and one student representative.

The duties of the working environment committee are described in the The Working Environment Act
